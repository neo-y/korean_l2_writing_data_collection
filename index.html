Error analysis of Korean Language Learners


Welcome to "the Error Analysis of Korean Language Learners", a web-based data collection project to gather writing data from beginner Korean learners. Before taking part in this study, please read all the information below and start with background questionnaire!

Purpose of the Project


The number of people who learn the Korean language is increasing annually. To teach Korean more effectively, the types of errors made by learners need to be analyzed and reflected in the teaching. This information can be fed into learning apps, which will enable more interactive learning. For this purpose, participants are asked to do two tasks which will be used for error analysis.

Participation


Qualification

You can participate if you satisfy ALL qualifications stated below.

  • Your korean proficiency level is between 1~3 (TOPIK)
  • You are fluent in English (at least B2)
  • You use only one primary language
  • You are able to type in Korean

Task

You are asked to participate in two tasks, which take about 60 minutes respectively. After completion of the first task, you have to do the second task within two weeks.

These are the procedure of the study:

  1. Gathering basic information about the participant
  2. Completion of the first task
  3. Completion of the second task

Further Information


Compensation

You will receive 15 Euros after completing the first task. After completing the second task, you will receive 15 Euros again.

Confidentiality

All information gathered in this study is completely confidential. Your personal information will be only used to contact you for further studies.
